About Us

Make your pool building experience a positive one.

Desert Tropics Custom Pools has been building pools in the Valley since 2001. Our President is also the Qualifying Party for our license. We don’t strive to build the most pools in the Valley, though we do strive to build the best pools in the Valley. We never use a template to put a pool in your yard. Each pool is custom designed by a professional, to meet your lifestyle and enhance the value of your home and your everyday enjoyment. We know all of our customers by name and our staff is dedicated to making sure that your needs are met. Most of our business comes to us by referral, and we strive to give every customer the best experience possible.

Our designers, Doug Slater and Deane Wilde have more than 30 years of combined experience in the Arizona swimming pool industry. Each of them oversees their projects from conception all the way thru to completion. So you never have to be concerned about being "handed off" to a company employee, or worse, to an outside contractor following the sale.

Our personal approach to pool building ensures continuity throughout the process, allowing you to relax in knowing your project will turn out exactly as you had envisioned.
